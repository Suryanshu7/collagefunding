Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships are awarded based on academic, athletic, or artistic merit, along with extracurricular activities and community service.

Need-Based Scholarships

Need-based scholarships are awarded to students who demonstrate financial need. These scholarships take into account the family's income, expenses, and other financial commitments.

Student-Specific Scholarships

Student-specific scholarships are aimed at groups of students who have a certain gender, race, religion, or medical history.

Career-Specific Scholarships

Career-specific scholarships are awarded to students who plan to pursue a specific field of study, such as nursing, engineering, or education.

College-Specific Scholarships

College-specific scholarships are offered by individual colleges and universities to attract talented students to their institution.

Creative Scholarships

Creative scholarships are awarded to students who excel in creative fields such as writing, music, and art.
